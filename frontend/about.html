BlinkNotes

Your Smart Hub for Study Notes

Problem

Students often struggle to find quality study materials for specific subjects or semesters. Notes are scattered across WhatsApp groups, Google Drive links, or personal devices — making them hard to search and organize. This leads to wasted time and the use of outdated resources.

Solution

BlinkNotes is a centralized online platform where students can upload notes with subject details, tags, and descriptions. They can search & browse by subject code, semester, keywords, or tags, and preview or download instantly — ensuring easy access and collaboration.

Languages & Technologies Used
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, React
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js, (Optional PHP)
  • Database: MongoDB, MySQL (optional)
  • File Storage: Google Drive API
  • Other Tools: Multer, Razorpay API (future integration)
Purpose

BlinkNotes is used to help students access study notes anytime, anywhere. It promotes collaboration and knowledge sharing, reduces duplication of effort, and serves as a digital library for academic resources.
