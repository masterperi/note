About BlinkNotes

Your Smart, Community-Driven Study Notes Hub

Brief Description

BlinkNotes is a smart, community-driven platform designed for students to upload, share, and browse study notes in an organized and interactive way.

It allows users to search notes by subject, semester, tags, or keywords, and view them instantly. Supports PDF uploads with detailed metadata like subject code, semester, description, and tags — making it easy to find exactly what you need.

Key Features

  • Browse Notes: Search and filter study notes quickly.
  • Upload Notes: Share your own notes with descriptions and tags.
  • Smart Search: AI-powered keyword search for quick results.
  • Community Sharing: Help other students while learning from them.
  • Responsive Design: Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

Mission

To create a collaborative and accessible learning hub where students can quickly find reliable study materials and contribute their own — making education more open and efficient.

The Problem

Students often struggle to find quality study materials for specific subjects or semesters. Notes are scattered across WhatsApp groups, Google Drive links, and personal devices — making them hard to search and organize. This wastes time and risks using outdated or irrelevant materials.

Solution

BlinkNotes provides a centralized platform where students can:

  • Upload notes with subject details, tags, and descriptions.
  • Search & browse by subject code, semester, keywords, or tags.
  • Preview or download notes instantly.

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js
  • Database: MongoDB
  • Other Tools: Multer (for file uploads in Node.js)

Purpose

  • Provide anytime, anywhere access to study notes.
  • Encourage collaboration and knowledge sharing.
  • Reduce duplication — share one high-quality version of notes.
  • Create a digital library of academic resources for students.
